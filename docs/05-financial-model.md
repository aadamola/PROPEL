## 3. Revenue Model & Projections

**Assumptions:**
- Blended retainer average ₦450k/client/month (mix of ₦250k–₦750k tiers and bundles).
- Client adds per month: 1–2 (M2–4, founder sales) → 2 (M5–12, referral+inbound kicking in) → 2–3 (Y2).
- Churn: 1 client per quarter from M6 (built in below).
- Launch projects: 1 in M6 (₦1.5m), then 1 per quarter (₦2m avg) from M9. OS builds: 1/month avg (₦500k) from M5.

**Year 1 totals (approx.):** revenue ₦52.6m · fixed costs ₦29.9m · pre-tax operating surplus ~₦22.6m (before partner performance distributions, VAT remittance handled separately, ~30% CIT provision on profits, and reinvestment).

**Break-even:** sustainably crossed at **month 5–6** (~8 retainers ≈ ₦3.6m MRR vs. ₦2.5m fixed costs). Cash cushion required until then is covered by capitalization.

### Year 2 (directional)

- Exit M24 at 22–25 retainers ≈ ₦8.5m–₦10m MRR + ₦8m–₦12m project revenue across the year.
- **Annualized run-rate target: ₦100m+** with EBITDA margin ≥30% after a proper salary layer (partners on market salaries + Head of Delivery).

### Sensitivity (know the downside)

| Scenario | Effect | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Client adds run 50% slower | Break-even slips to M9–10; capitalization still covers it | Cut sales & marketing discretionary, delay Hire 2, partners stay unpaid longer |
| Churn doubles (2/quarter) | M12 MRR ~₦4.5m, still profitable but flat | Trigger: churn ≥2/qtr → mandatory pricing/packaging review + client success sprint |
| Naira devalues 30% | Tool costs +~₦120k/mo | Contractual annual price review; shift stack toward NGN-billed tools |

## 5. Financial Controls (non-negotiable from day 1)

1. Month-in-advance billing; automated service pause at 7 days overdue.
2. Weekly cash position check (Partner A); monthly P&L vs. this model with variance notes.
3. Separate tax sub-account: sweep 7.5% VAT + 25% profit provision on every receipt — never spendable.
4. No single client >15% of MRR by M12 (concentration rule).
5. Tool spend capped at 8% of revenue; audit the stack quarterly and kill unused seats.
6. Partner draws only after payroll, tax sweep, and a 2-month fixed-cost reserve are covered.
