# Propel — 24-Month Roadmap

Owners: **A** = Managing Partner, Growth & Strategy · **B** = Managing Partner, Market & Delivery (Adedamola) · **Team** = hires.
Each phase has an exit gate — do not advance until the gate is met.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

**Goal:** legally real, visibly credible, ready to sell.

| # | Milestone | Owner | Done when |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.1 | Sign partnership term sheet (equity, vesting, roles, capital) | A+B | Signed by both partners |
| 0.2 | CAC incorporation filed + TIN + corporate bank account | B | Certificate + account live |
| 0.3 | Brand v1: logo, colors, one-page site with WhatsApp CTA, propel handles secured (IG/TikTok/LinkedIn/YouTube) | A | Site live, handles secured |
| 0.4 | AI delivery stack configured + prompt library v1 (see docs/06) | A | 3 sample client deliverables produced end-to-end |
| 0.5 | Service packages + rate card + MSA/SOW templates finalized | A+B | Lawyer-reviewed MSA ready to send |
| 0.6 | Prospect list: 100 named targets (40 agents, 30 surveyors/valuers, 20 brokerages, 10 developers) with warm-intro paths mapped | B | List in CRM with tiering |
| 0.7 | Propel's own content engine starts: 3 posts/week educating realtors on marketing | A+B | 2 weeks of content shipped |

**Exit gate:** company exists, MSA ready, 100-target list built, our own content publishing consistently.

## Phase 1 — First Revenue (Months 2–4)

**Goal:** 4–6 paying retainer clients; prove delivery and collect proof.

| # | Milestone | Owner | Done when |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1.1 | Founding Client Offer executed (5 clients at 20% off for 6-mo term + testimonial rights) | B | 5 signed |
| 1.2 | First ₦1m collected revenue | A+B | Cash in bank |
| 1.3 | Hire 1: Content/AI Producer (₦250k–₦350k/mo) | A | Onboarded, owning 60% of content production |
| 1.4 | Delivery playbook v1: onboarding checklist, content calendar SOP, reporting template | A | Any client onboarded in <5 days |
| 1.5 | Weekly client scorecard automated (leads, DMs, inspections booked) | A | Reports send without manual work |
| 1.6 | First case study with hard numbers | B | Published on site + socials |
| 1.7 | Attend/speak at 2 industry gatherings (NIESV Lagos branch, agent network events) | B | 20+ new qualified contacts |

**KPIs:** ≥₦1.8m MRR · client onboarding <5 days · content delivered on time ≥95% · Propel channels: 2,500+ followers, 10+ inbound leads/mo.

**Exit gate:** 4+ retainers, 1 public case study, delivery running without partner heroics.

## Phase 2 — Break-Even & Systems (Months 5–8)

**Goal:** break-even; land first developer Launch project; sales stops depending on founders' personal networks.

| # | Milestone | Owner | Done when |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2.1 | 8–10 retainer clients / ≥₦3.5m MRR (break-even) | A+B | 2 consecutive months above break-even |
| 2.2 | Hire 2: Media Buyer/Automation (₦300k–₦400k/mo) | A | Paid campaigns fully delegated |
| 2.3 | First Propel Launch project (off-plan development, ₦1.5m–₦3m fee) | A | Signed + delivered; units-sold data captured |

| 2.5 | Production partner network: 3 vetted videographer/drone crews on per-shoot rates | B | 2 shoots delivered via partners |
| 2.6 | AI lead-qualification bot productized as Propel OS setup offer | A | Sold to 3 clients |

**KPIs:** MRR ≥₦3.5m · gross margin ≥55% · churn ≤1 client/quarter · ≥30% of new clients from referral/inbound.

**Exit gate:** profitable for 2 straight months; a client has been sold, onboarded, and served with <20% founder hands-on time.

## Phase 3 — Scale the Engine (Months 9–14)

**Goal:** ₦6m+ MRR, first full pod running without partners in delivery, category-brand momentum.

| # | Milestone | Owner | Done when |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3.1 | 12–15 retainers; blended ₦450k+/client | A+B | ₦6m MRR sustained |

**Exit gate:** one pod runs delivery end-to-end; brand generates inbound weekly.

## Standing Cadence (from Month 1)

- **Weekly (Mon):** partner pipeline + delivery standup, 45 min.
- **Weekly (Fri):** client scorecards auto-sent; flag any account with lead decline.
- **Monthly:** P&L review vs. model (docs/05); churn/NPS review; one pricing/packaging experiment.
- **Quarterly:** strategy offsite (half-day), roadmap re-gate, client QBRs.
