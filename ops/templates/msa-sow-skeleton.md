# Master Services Agreement + SOW — Skeleton (for lawyer review)

*The contract armor referenced across docs/01 §6, docs/08 §"mitigations", and every offering. NOT legal advice — a CAC-accredited lawyer converts this to final form (favor-rate review per docs/07). Nothing goes to a client before that review. Every clause below exists for a specific documented reason.*

## MSA core clauses (the non-negotiables)

1. **Parties & scope.** Propel Digital ("Propel") ↔ Client. Services defined per attached SOW(s); this MSA governs all SOWs.
2. **Facts warranty (rule 2 armor).** Client warrants in writing the accuracy of ALL property facts supplied for marketing (price, title status, dimensions, availability, amenities, payment plans). Propel markets only client-warranted facts; **Propel does not verify title and provides no legal or valuation opinion.**
3. **Indemnity.** Client indemnifies Propel against claims arising from the client's listings, property facts, or transactions with buyers.
4. **Limitation of liability.** Propel's total liability capped at fees paid in the [3] months preceding the claim; no liability for indirect/consequential loss, lost profits, or platform actions (e.g., Meta policy changes) outside Propel's control.
5. **Payment terms.** Monthly in advance · services pause automatically at [7] days overdue (no penalty to Propel) · 3-month minimum on retainers, then rolling with 30 days' notice · annual price review indexed to tooling/FX (docs/03).
6. **Ad spend.** Paid by Client directly to platforms. Propel never receives, holds, or disburses ad funds.
7. **AI disclosure & QA.** Client acknowledges Propel uses AI systems under human quality control per Propel's published QA standard; automated assistants disclose their nature to end users on request; Concierge answers only from the client's warranted knowledge base and escalates outside it.
8. **Data protection (NDPA).** Roles defined (Client = controller of buyer data collected on its behalf; Propel = processor): lawful-basis/consent capture on forms, processing limited to the services, no sale of data, breach notification within [72h], deletion/return on termination. Client buyer-PII is not transferred to third-country AI APIs (docs/13 §3).
9. **IP & licence.** Propel retains its methods, prompts, templates, and systems. Client owns final approved deliverables for its business use, licensed on full payment. Propel may reference non-confidential work per clause 10.
10. **Testimonial & case-study rights.** [Founding clients: included — video testimonial + case study with real numbers, subject to client approval of the final cut. Standard: opt-in.]
11. **Term & termination.** Either party on 30 days' notice post-minimum; immediate termination for non-payment (after pause), unremedied breach, or **Propel's vetting exit: Propel may terminate immediately if property documentation proves materially misrepresented** (we don't market disputed land — rule 5).
12. **Kill switch.** Client may pause automated systems (Concierge/campaigns) with one written (WhatsApp) instruction, effective within [1] business day.
13. **Governing law & disputes.** Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria; [Lagos] courts / [mediation-first] — lawyer to advise.
