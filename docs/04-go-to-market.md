# Propel — Go-To-Market Plan

**Objective:** 10 paying retainer clients by month 4; ≥50% of pipeline inbound by month 12.

**GTM thesis:** In Lagos real estate, trust travels through people, and attention travels through content. So we run two engines simultaneously — **warm-network founder sales** (fast revenue) and **public proof-of-work content** (compounding inbound) — and we make our own marketing the demo.

---

## 1. The First 10 Clients (Months 1–4)

### Step 1 — The list (Week 2)
100 named targets in the CRM, tiered:
- **Tier A (20):** warm intros exist through Partner B's network — agents/surveyors already spending on marketing or visibly active on Instagram.
- **Tier B (50):** active Lagos agents/firms with weak-but-existing digital presence (they feel the pain; the gap is visible).
- **Tier C (30):** developers and brokerages — longer cycles, bigger tickets; start conversations now to close in months 3–6.

### Step 2 — The Founding Client Offer
20% off any retainer for 6 months, capped at 5 clients, in exchange for testimonial + case-study rights and a weekly feedback call. Scarcity is real and stated: *"We're taking five founding clients before prices go to rate card."*

### Step 3 — The sales motion (WhatsApp-native)
1. **Warm intro or DM** referencing something specific in their marketing (we send a 60-second Loom-style audit video — AI-assisted to produce in <15 minutes).
2. **The Audit Call (30 min, remote):** walk through 3 gaps + what fixing them is worth in inspections/closings. Give real value even if they never buy.
3. **Same-day proposal:** one-page package recommendation from templates — never a bespoke deck at this stage.
4. **Close on WhatsApp**, sign via e-signature, invoice with Paystack link. Onboarding call within 48 hours of payment.

**Founder sales math (assumption):** 100 targets → 40 audit calls → 12 proposals → 6–8 closes at 20–25% proposal-to-close for warm, 10% for cold. Partner B owns relationships; Partner A owns proposals/pricing and closes developer deals jointly.

### Step 4 — Land small, expand fast
It's easier to upsell a delighted ₦250k client to ₦450k than to close cold at ₦450k. Every account gets a 90-day expansion target logged at onboarding.

## 2. The Inbound Engine (Month 1 onward, compounds forever)

**Propel's own channels are the product demo.** If we can't market ourselves brilliantly, why would anyone pay us to market them?

| Channel | Play | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram/TikTok** | "Real estate marketing clinic": teardowns of anonymized listings, before/after content makeovers, "why this Reel sold a duplex in Ajah" breakdowns | 4–5/week |
| **LinkedIn** | Partner A publishes market-analysis + agency-building content targeting surveyors/valuers & developers (they're on LinkedIn; agents aren't) | 2–3/week |
| **YouTube** | Monthly deep-dives: "Diaspora buyer psychology", "Marketing an off-plan estate in 2026" | 1–2/month |
| **WhatsApp Channel** | Weekly "Propel Signal": 3 tactical marketing tips for realtors; CTA to audit call | Weekly |
| **Email** | Same content repackaged; list built from lead magnet | Weekly |

**Lead magnet:** *"The Lagos Real Estate Marketing Playbook"* (free PDF, gated by WhatsApp/email) — showcases exactly how we think; qualifies leads by who bothers to read it.

**Annual flagship:** "State of Real Estate Marketing in Nigeria" report (Phase 3) — the PR and authority anchor.

## 3. Trust & Community (the Lagos multiplier)

1. **Industry bodies:** show up consistently at NIESV Lagos branch events, REDAN gatherings, and agent-network meetups. Goal: Partner B speaking on "AI marketing for real estate" within 6 months. Surveyors/valuers are a community that talks — one respected firm as a client opens ten doors.
2. **Referral program:** 10% of the first 3 months' fees to the referrer (agents understand commission viscerally). Promoted openly to clients and industry friends.
3. **Strategic partners (non-competing, same customer):** property lawyers, title-verification startups, mortgage brokers, proptech portals, photographers who don't do strategy. Reciprocal referral deals; co-hosted webinars.
4. **Quarterly Propel Roundtable:** invite-only dinner for 10–12 top agents/developers. Costs ~₦400k; one closed deal pays for a year of dinners.

---

## 4. Paid Acquisition (only after proof, Month 5+)

- Retarget site/profile visitors with case-study video ads (small budget, ₦150k–₦300k/mo).
- Lead-gen campaign targeting "real estate agent" interest/behavior audiences in Lagos with the Playbook lead magnet.
- Rule: paid never exceeds 20% of pipeline — this business closes on trust, and paid buys attention, not trust.

---

## 5. Sales Infrastructure

- **CRM from day 1** (HubSpot free tier or NG-friendly alternative): every target, stage, and next-step logged; weekly pipeline review Monday standup.
- **Assets needed before first outreach:** rate card PDF, MSA/SOW, 3 sample content packs (create for fictional listings), audit-video template, proposal template, onboarding checklist.
- **KPIs:** audit calls booked/week (target 5+ by month 2) · proposal→close ≥25% warm · CAC <₦100k blended · time-to-onboard <5 days.
