# Propel — Bootstrap Plan (v2, supersedes capital assumptions in docs/01 & 05)

*Revised July 2026 after founder discovery. This is now the operative plan; docs/01–06 remain the destination, this is the road that actually starts from where we stand.*

## What changed

| Assumption (v1) | Reality (discovered) | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| ₦9.5m partner capital | **₦500k total** | Zero fixed-cost structure; revenue funds everything from client 1 |
| Full-time founders | **~20 hrs/week (Justin)** | Delivery capacity caps at 3–4 clients until revenue buys time/help |
| Network unknown | **Warm across agents, surveyors/valuers, AND developers** | Founder-sales engine is stronger than modeled — this is our real capital |
| Some assets exist | **Blank slate** | Phase 0 stands, but stripped to essentials |

**The new thesis:** Propel starts as a *profitable service practice*, not a funded agency. The AI partner (me) carries production; Justin carries relationships and 20 focused hours; the first ₦1.5m–₦3m of client revenue — not investor capital — funds incorporation extras, tooling, and the first hire. Nothing in docs/01–06 is abandoned; the sequence is re-cut so every naira spent is preceded by a naira earned.

## 3. Revised sequence (replaces docs/02 pacing; gates unchanged in spirit)

**Sprint 1 (weeks 1–2) — Sell before we build.**
CAC filing submitted · pick 10 Tier-A names (mix all three segments) · I produce 10 audit teardowns + videos scripts · Justin sends 10 audit videos → books 5 calls. *No brand-building beyond handles + one-pager. Proof of demand before polish.*

**Sprint 2 (weeks 3–6) — First ₦750k.**
Close 2–3 founding clients (Presence Starter ₦200k founding rate / Growth ₦360k founding rate). Month-in-advance payment lands *before* onboarding starts. Deliver brilliantly at 3-client load. Target: **₦600k–₦1m collected by week 6** — capital doubled without dilution.

**Months 2–4 — The developer swing.**
With 2 case studies forming, Justin's developer contacts get the Launch Lite pitch (₦1.5m). **One Launch Lite = 3× our starting capital** and funds: Ltd upgrade + lawyer, 6 months of tooling, and the equipment kit. Retainer base target: 4 clients / ₦1.2m–₦1.5m MRR — the realistic ceiling at 20 hrs/week.

**Months 4–6 — Buy back time, then scale.**
First hire (Content/AI Producer, ₦250k/mo) ONLY after: 3 consecutive months ≥₦1.5m MRR *and* 2-month salary reserve banked. The hire doubles capacity to 7–8 clients → from here, docs/02 Phase 2 resumes as written, self-funded.

**Break-even reality:** with fixed costs ≈ ₦80k/month, we are profitable from client #1. The constraint was never costs — it's hours. Every decision optimizes for: *revenue per Justin-hour, then buy Justin-hours back with revenue.*

## 4. Network play (all three segments warm — sequence them, don't blitz them)

1. **Weeks 1–6: agents/realtors first.** Fastest closes, shortest sales cycle, immediate case-study material. 2 of our first 3 clients come from here.
2. **Weeks 3–8: one flagship surveyor/valuer firm.** Slower, stickier, unlocks the NIESV referral room. Give this one disproportionate care — it's a door, not a deal.
3. **Months 2–4: developers.** Approach only after 2 case studies exist; pitch Launch Lite with proof in hand. One close changes the year.

## 5. Risks specific to bootstrap mode

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| 20 hrs/wk overruns → delivery slips → churn | Hard cap at 4 clients until Hire 1; I absorb all absorbable work; Monday review tracks Justin-hours like cash |
| ₦500k gone before first close | Spend gate: only CAC + tooling released up-front; everything else unlocks on first payment received |
| Founding discounts anchor prices low | Founding rate is explicitly temporary, in writing, tied to testimonial rights; rate card (docs/03) is the published truth |
| Sole-founder key-person risk (one human carries all relationships, unlimited liability as sole prop) | Tight MSA liability clauses from client #1 (docs/08); Ltd upgrade on defined triggers; playbooks in repo so the business survives any pause |

*Success metric for this document: it becomes obsolete by month 4 because revenue restored the original plan.*
