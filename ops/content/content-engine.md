# Propel — Content Engine

*How our own channels run: the machine that makes us the best-marketed marketing agency in Lagos real estate. I (AI partner) draft everything; Justin approves and posts (or schedules via Metricool/Buffer). Target human time: ≤30 min/day.*

## The weekly rhythm (Propel's own channels)

| Day | Slot | Format | Pillar |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Teardown Monday | IG carousel + TikTok talking points | Authority (audit clinic) |
| Tue | — | Story only: poll/question | Engagement |
| Wed | Playbook Wednesday | Reel/TikTok (30–45s script) | Education |

| Fri | Proof Friday | Carousel or Reel | Social proof / results |
| Sat | Market Saturday | Static + long caption | Market insight (Lagos data) |
| Sun | rest | — | — |

**Engagement block (daily, 10 min, human):** meaningful comments on 10 target accounts (big NG real estate pages + Tier-A prospects), reply to every comment/DM on ours. This is where followers actually come from.

## Production loop (weekly, batched)

1. **Sunday:** I generate the full week's pack (copy + scripts + design briefs) → repo + WhatsApp to Justin
2. **Monday review (15 min):** approve/edit in our partner session
3. **Justin films any talking-head clips in ONE 30-min batch** (phone, natural light, 3 scripts back-to-back)
4. I finalize captions/design specs; Justin (or scheduler) queues the week
5. **Friday:** I log what performed → next week's pack adapts. Metrics that count: profile visits, link taps, DMs started, audit calls booked.

## Voice rules

Direct, generous, numbers-forward, Lagos-fluent. We give away real tactics (the audit call sells the *doing*, not the *knowing*). Never punch down at agents' current marketing — critique patterns, not people. English with natural Lagos inflection; no forced slang.
