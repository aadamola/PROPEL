## The deal shape (recommendation)

This is a **commission mandate**, not a retainer — a new revenue line ("Propel Partners") and exactly the developer swing docs/07 planned for, arriving early.

**Recommended structure — propose A, accept B:**
- **Option A (preferred):** Hybrid — modest monthly marketing fee (₦300k–₦500k covering content + campaigns for their project) **plus** success commission per unit/plot sold through Propel-generated leads (market range for external marketers in Lagos new-builds is typically 2.5–5%; anchor at 5%, settle ≥3%). Fee covers our costs; commission is the upside.
- **Option B (acceptable):** Pure commission at the higher band (≥5%) with a written 6-month exclusive on digital lead generation, developer covers ad spend directly, and a 12-month lead-attribution window (WhatsApp/CRM tagging proves source).
- **Walk away if:** pure commission at low rates with no exclusivity, no attribution mechanism, or any resistance to documentation checks. "We'll pay you when it sells" with no protections is how marketers work for free.

**Attribution is the whole game in commission deals:** every Propel lead enters via our tracked WhatsApp link/landing page and is logged in CRM before handoff to their sales team — written into the agreement, with commission payable on any logged lead that closes within the window.

**Social proof value (why this matters beyond cash):** "Marketing partner to Shalom Park Estate (IFT Realty)" on our site, socials and pitches — a named, press-covered developer relationship is stronger proof than any follower count, exactly the credibility our Featured Projects section is built to hold. Testimonial + case-study rights go in the agreement.
