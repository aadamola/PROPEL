# Demo 3 — Propel Launch for Shalom Park (90-Day Campaign Concept)

*The estate launched in 2024; this is a RE-LAUNCH play: "Shalom Park — Chapter Two" (new phase/remaining inventory/construction milestone — hook chosen with IFT from what's actually true).*

## Positioning line (concept)

**"Built like someone plans to live here."** — infrastructure-proof over luxury-vague. Every asset ladders to it: underground power, water treatment, covered drains, documentation. In a market drowning in golden-render promises, engineering-forward is the differentiated trust story.

## 90 days, 3 acts

**Act 1 — Credibility (weeks 1–4):** documentation story ("papers on the table" content [VERIFY docs first]), infrastructure Reels series, developer-story feature (IFT's 2005-vision narrative from their public About page), retargeting pixel warm-up. *Goal: make the estate the "safe hands" choice in Abijo conversations.*

**Act 2 — Demand (weeks 5–10):** paid push on all three audiences (02-leads-demo) · diaspora virtual-inspection events (scheduled group walkthroughs, timezone-friendly) · payment-plan explainer campaign · agent-network activation (brokers get a co-branded asset kit — they sell for the commission, we arm them).

**Act 3 — Urgency, honestly (weeks 11–13):** verified remaining-inventory counts only · price-adjustment notice if one is genuinely scheduled · "last virtual inspection cohort of the quarter." **We never fake scarcity — fake urgency is fraud-adjacent and this brand can't touch it.**

## Deliverables in the Launch fee
Campaign strategy + this creative platform · 30+ assets (Reels, carousels, ads, landing page, email/WhatsApp sequences) · media management · weekly sales-team lead reviews · Concierge integration · end-of-campaign report with per-unit attribution.

## What we'd need from IFT
