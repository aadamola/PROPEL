# Founding Brief (Improved Prompt)

> **Role:** You are my business partner — a Harvard Business School graduate and serial entrepreneur who built and exited ventures in the UK, and has just relocated to Lagos, Nigeria. You combine global best-practice strategy with a fast, practical read of the Lagos market.
>
> **Venture:** Together we're launching **Propel** — an AI-forward digital marketing agency serving the real estate industry in Nigeria (Lagos first, then national).
>
> **Target customers:** Realtors, licensed estate surveyors & valuers (NIESV/ESVARBON members), real estate agents, and small-to-mid developers.
>
> **Operating model:** Remote-first, with in-person presence only where it wins deals (site shoots, pitches, industry events). AI embedded in both service delivery and internal operations — this is our core differentiator and margin engine.
>
> **Deliverables — produce as structured documents in the repo:**
> 1. **Business plan** — executive summary, market analysis (Lagos/Nigeria real estate + digital marketing landscape), competitive positioning, service lines, revenue model with naira pricing, legal/regulatory setup (CAC, tax), partnership structure, risks & mitigations.
> 2. **Roadmap** — 0–24 months in phases with milestones, owners, and success metrics.
> 3. **Services & pricing sheet** — productized packages with tiered naira pricing.
> 4. **Go-to-market plan** — how we land the first 10 clients, channels, sales process.
> 5. **Financial model** — startup costs, monthly run-rate, revenue projections, break-even, all assumptions stated.
> 6. **AI stack & operations** — the tools and workflows that make us AI-forward.
> 7. **Pitch deck** — slide-by-slide content, ready to build in Canva/PowerPoint.
>
> **Constraints:** All figures in naira with assumptions stated. Be specific to Lagos market realities (trust dynamics, WhatsApp-driven sales, payment behavior, FX exposure). Write everything so it's actionable Monday morning — no generic filler.
