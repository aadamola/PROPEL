# Demo 4 — Propel Studio for Shalom Park (One Shoot Day → One Month of Assets)

*One 6-hour site day with our partner crew (videographer + drone pilot), engineered so a single visit feeds Presence, Leads, Launch AND Concierge for weeks. This plan is the demo: it shows IFT we think in systems, not shoots.*

## The walkthrough film (2–3 min hero asset + cutdowns)

**Script skeleton (voiceover, faceless per our production standard):**
1. **Open on the gate** — "Twenty minutes from Ajah, a different philosophy of estate."
2. **The streets** — steady gimbal walk: paved roads, covered drains ("watch where the water goes — in Lagos, that's the tell").
3. **The invisible sell** — cutaway graphics over footage: underground power routing, water treatment plant exterior ("the things you can't photoshop into a render").
4. **Unit walkthroughs** — one per type [2-bed condo / 4-bed semi-D / 5-bed detached], 20 seconds each: light, space, finishing close-ups. Doors opened on camera, meters shown — diaspora buyers study details locals skip.
5. **Documentation beat** — sales office, physical files on the table [VERIFY docs before filming this beat].
6. **Close on residents/security** — "Built like someone plans to live here." CTA: virtual inspection.

## Drone shot list (10 shots)
Sunrise establishing orbit · top-down street grid (drainage visible) · gate-to-estate approach line · corridor context shot toward Lekki-Epe expressway [verify sightline] · each unit-type cluster · water plant + power infrastructure · construction-progress zones (honest status) · golden-hour closing pull-back.

## Stills pack (40 edited)
Per unit type: 6 exteriors, 6 interiors · infrastructure set · lifestyle details (playground, walkways) · documentation/sales-office set. All geotagged, all usable across ads, portal listings, and the landing page.

## Output math (the pitch line)
One shoot day → hero film + 8 Reels cutdowns + 40 stills + drone library + every Concierge "can I see X?" answer = **a month of content from six hours of access.** Studio cost per rate card: ₦180k video + ₦100k drone + ₦90k stills (bundled into Launch fee if the mandate signs).
