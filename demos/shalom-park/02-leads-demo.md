# Demo 2 — Propel Leads for Shalom Park

*Campaign architecture + 6 ready ad variants. Spend paid by IFT Realty directly to Meta/Google (our standard). Landing page demo: landing-demo.html in this folder.*

## Funnel

Meta/IG ads (cold + retargeting) → landing page (landing-demo.html) → WhatsApp click-to-chat → **Concierge qualifies 24/7** → hot leads to IFT sales team with context summary → inspection (virtual for diaspora, physical for Lagos) → close. Every lead tagged Propel-attributed in CRM (this powers the commission model).

## Audiences (concept)

1. **Diaspora prime:** UK/US/Canada Nigerians 30–55, homeownership + investment interests, engaged with Nigerian real estate content. Creative leads with process-trust, priced in ₦ AND £/$ equivalents [PLACEHOLDER rates].
2. **Lagos upgraders:** Lekki–Ajah corridor renters/owners 28–50, income proxies. Creative leads with commute + infrastructure.
3. **Retargeting:** site visitors, IG engagers, video 50%+ viewers — payment-plan and urgency angles [only true scarcity: actual remaining units, verified].
